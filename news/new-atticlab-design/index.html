Announcing The Launch Of Our Newly Redesigned Website

June 12, 2018

We’re excited to announce the launch of our redesigned website. The Attic Lab’s website www.atticlab.net has been completely redesigned and upgraded.

Our goal was to provide our visitors with a simple way to discover more about our expertise, our projects and solutions we realize for our clients from all over the world.

First of all, it’s fully adaptive, meaning that the website is now compatible with any device, like desktops, laptops, netbooks, tablets etc. Also, it is more aesthetically pleasing and easy to use due to the completely new UI, lighter color palette and modern fonts.

The website’s content has been thoroughly reviewed as well, thereby, offering the latest news and the most critical information to our visitors.

Our social media accounts have been updated, too. All the visuals, including profiles images and covers were redesigned in accordance with the new style of the website. Here you can find a full list of our social media account you’re more than welcome to join:

Steemit: https://steemit.com/@attic-lab

Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/atticlab

Linkedin: https://www.linkedin.com/in/attic-lab-5ab9a2129/

Twitter: https://twitter.com/atticlab_it

Medium: https://medium.com/@attic.it.lab

We are genuinely proud of our website redesign and the content our website has to offer you today. We hope you will find the navigation process more efficient and will be able to find whatever you are looking in the utmost quickly and simple way.

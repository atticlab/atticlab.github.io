Attic Lab has successfully launched a new project - Cryptagio.com

June 1, 2018

We are proud to announce the successful launch of our recent project - Cryptagio digital asset exchange.

Our team has a proven track record in investment, banking and trading sectors as well as extensive skill set in design, engineering and marketing. We joined our forces, consolidated insights and co-create token-based solutions that ensure consistent development and stability of the platform.

Cryptagio was built to accommodate those, striving to get ahead and take hold in today’s crypto market, regardless of their level of experience. Launched on 10 April 2018, currently Cryptagio supports 11 most popular cryptocurrencies on today’s market.

Cryptagio incorporates incomparably fast trading mechanisms, distinctively clear, user-friendly interface, multi-factor security system, and responsive 24⁄7 support infrastructure. Through acceleration of the exchange process, high liquidity, and easy-to-use API, the exchange will meet the needs of beginners and experienced crypto holders.

“We are committed to ensure a perfectly functional and consistently stable trading environment, allowing our users to create and develop much potent crypto portfolios in a strikingly fast, easy and safe way”, says Attic Lab’s CEO Sergii Vasylchuk.

To bring more value to its users, Cryptagio has designed their own token. Unlike many others, Cryptagio token (CPG) was created with a single purpose of providing beneficial terms and gainful experiences within the platform.

The platform is also going to introduce additional useful features, such as a mobile app, support of fiat money, FIX API, margin trading, PAMM etc.

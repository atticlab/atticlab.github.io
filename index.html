We created this page to make voting for block producers quick and secure.
Just follow these 5 simple steps:

Step 1

Create a new folder on your PC

Step 2

Download eos.js v14.1.1 script from official EOS github account to this folder

Step 3

Paste your account name into the input below and choose the amount of EOS to delegate (if available).
We'll delegate the chosen amount 50/50 to CPU and NET.
Press 'generate' and save html file into the same folder

Step 4

Submit required fields and click generate. You'll get a signed transaction

Step 5

Paste signed transaction to form and press submit

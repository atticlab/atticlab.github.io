I’m Sergey Vasylchuk, a co-founder and CEO of Attic Lab. We are an innovative fintech company that has got the most inspired professionals in banking, IT and crypto industries. It is a great honor for me to lead the team of young experts, determined to reinvent the current financial system.

Our goals may seem beyond possible or even crazy, but I’m convinced this is the only way to change the world and make impact.

We participate in disruptive projects only. Though we are young, we work with banks and businesses across the world, from Africa to Asia. Our expertise allows us to feel confident on both traditional financial and dynamic crypto markets. Our software is certified by Central Banks, and many financial institutions use it already today.
